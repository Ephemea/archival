# Archical
Ephemea Archival is a privately owned, no-profit archival effort. Ephemea Archival bases itself around collecting and preserving military documents &amp; books, though also including civilian books.

# Mission
The long term mission is to preserve and hold military history for many years, ensuring that research and interest in such provided will be avaliable for years to come. 

# Access
Ephemea Archival is currently privately maintained. Access is limited and controlled by the staff. To gain access to elligible documents or books, please open an issue on the GitHub repository or @Ephemea on Mewster. NOTICE::: Email requests will eventually replace the current methods, please account for the future.
