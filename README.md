# Archival
Ephemea Archival is a privately owned, no-profit archival effort. Ephemea Archival bases itself around collecting and preserving military documents &amp; books, though also including civilian books.

# Mission
The long term mission is to preserve and hold military history for many years, ensuring that research and interest in such provided will be avaliable for years to come. 

# Access & Requirements
Ephemea Archival is currently privately maintained. Access is limited and controlled by the staff. To gain access to elligible documents or books, please email at @ephemea.proton.me. Responses & following access will take as high as 2 weeks if busy. Please note current holidays and weekends before applying to prevent long waits. 

Applicants must state their affiliation (e.g., independent researcher, university student, historian, author).
For sensitive documents and or books, require a form of identification (passport, government ID, etc ..) and an institutional email address (e.g., .edu or official organizational emails) as valid proof they are elligible.
