# Archival
Ephemea Archival is a privately owned, non-profit archival effort. Ephemea Archival bases itself around collecting and preserving military documents &amp; books, though also including civilian books.

# Mission
The long term mission is to preserve and hold military history for many years, ensuring that research and interest in these materials will be preserved for years to come. 

# Access & Requirements
Ephemea Archival is currently privately maintained. Access is limited and controlled by the staff. To gain access to eligible documents or books, please email at @ephemea.proton.me. Response times and subsequent access approval may take up to 2 weeks depending on current volume. Please take national holidays and weekends into account when submitting a request.

Applicants must state their affiliation (e.g., independent researcher, university student, historian, author).
For sensitive documents and or books, we require a form of identification (passport, government ID, etc ..) and an institutional email address (e.g., .edu or official organizational emails) as valid proof they are eligible.
