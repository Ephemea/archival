About

Ephemea Archival is a privately owned archival effort. Ephemea Archival bases itself around collecting and preserving military documents & books, though also including civilian books. The long term mission is to preserve and hold military history for many years, ensuring that research and interest in such provided will be avaliable for years to come.

This is a private personal archive for historical and educational purposes only. Not affiliated with any government agency. All rights reserved.
